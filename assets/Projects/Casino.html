The Great Casino


A cyberpunk themed level design

                        
Overview

                        This was a practise Level for me where 
                        I tried to guide the player with subtle details like player crowds, 
                        guiding lines and lightning.
                        
                        Inspired from the Black Sapphire from CP77: Phantom Liberty, 
                        the player here is supposed to go undetected, blended in as a visitor, 
                        with the goal of reaching the Grand Casino and enter it to reach the next milestone.
                        
                        This level is easily structured, and 
                        I thought of something like a tutorial level, 
                        that's why there is a lack of guards here.

                        
Genre:
RPG, First-Person RPG
                        
Engine:
Unreal Engine 5.3

                    
                        
Level Preproduction

                        I drew a 2D Layout in Krita.

                        Inspiration for that level brought me the Phantom Liberty DLC, 
                        with the Black Sapphire as a Location of main interest.

                        The level is structured in isles, 
                        an idea that came after I played SWTOR on the planet “Narshadar”, 
                        which is a Megapolis with playable grounds also structured as Isles.
                    
                        
3D Scene

                        After that I used the UE pre-installed „Level Prototype“ assets to block out the environment.
                        Next I decided to set the scene at night, 
                        there I can navigate the player by placing lights and 
                        generate a stunning effect of the neon lights (especially the neon beacon of the casino itself).
